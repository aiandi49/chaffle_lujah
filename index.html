How We Got Our Name

One of the questions we get most often is...

"Okay, but how do you pronounce it?" 😅

The name started as a joke, but the more we said it, the more it stuck. Now it's become a reminder that good food should be fun, a little unexpected, and maybe even worthy of a tiny happy dance.

So whether you came for The OG, Holy Beet, Plantain Jane, or The Benny… welcome to Chaffle-lujah.

We know. We made it weird. ✨

The Basics

So... what's a chaffle?

A chaffle is a savory, cheese-based waffle — crispy on the outside, soft in the middle, and endlessly customizable. We take that simple idea and build it up with wholesome, real ingredients: cheese, eggs, sweet potatoes, plantains, and whatever's fresh that week.

The result? Little golden creations that feel like comfort food and taste like something you've never quite had before.

Menu

Signature Toppings

Thoughtfully layered with fresh, flavorful ingredients that turn every chaffle into its own little masterpiece. From creamy and savory to bright and tangy, each combination is designed to complement the crispy, cheesy base—not cover it up.

The Holy Beet

Proof that vegetables can absolutely steal the show. Your choice of our golden, crispy chaffles is topped with creamy whipped feta, vibrant pickled beets, fresh sunflower microgreens, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. Every bite is the perfect balance of tangy, creamy, earthy, and sweet—fresh, colorful, and surprisingly craveable.

What's inside: Your choice of chaffle, whipped feta, pickled beets, sunflower microgreens, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Vegetarian Naturally Gluten-Free

The Benny

A breakfast classic with the Chaffle-lujah treatment. Start with your choice of our golden, crispy chaffles, then choose turkey bacon or Canadian bacon to make it your own. We top it all with a fried egg, silky hollandaise, and a handful of fresh pea microgreens for a bright, peppery finish. It's rich, comforting, and just fancy enough to make any morning feel like brunch.

What's inside: Your choice of chaffle, turkey bacon or Canadian bacon, fried egg, hollandaise, and pea microgreens.
